Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of cooked bacon
  • 5 tablespoons of soft butter, divided
  • 4 slices bread
  • 4 slices cheese
  • 4 slices Tomato

Instructions

  1. Spread about 1 tablespoon butter into the bottom of a large skillet
  2. Spread 1tablespoon onto the one side of each side of each slice of bread
  3. Place the 4 slices of bread butter side down onto the large skillet or flay grill
  4. Top with 1 slice of cheese,2 slices of tomato and 2 slices cooked bacon,then 1more slice of cheese
  5. Cover a slice of bread
  6. Fry over medium turning once with a spatula until golden brown on both sides
